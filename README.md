## 📌 Business Objective  

To analyze supermarket transaction data in order to identify revenue drivers, customer behavior patterns, branch performance differences, payment preferences, and temporal sales trends.  

---

## ❓ Business Questions  

1. Which product line contributes the most to total revenue?  
2. How does branch performance differ?  
3. How does membership affect spending?  
4. What payment methods dominate per category?  
5. How do weekday and weekend sales differ?  

---

## 🧹 Data Preparation  

- No missing values detected  
- No duplicate transactions found  
- Proper data type formatting applied (datetime conversion for Date & Time columns)  
- Dataset confirmed clean and analysis-ready  

---

## 📈 Key Insights  

- Food & Beverages is the highest revenue contributor, though category revenue distribution remains balanced.  
- Branch performance is relatively even, indicating stable operational management.  
- Membership slightly increases average transaction value.  
- Payment preferences vary across categories, with strong digital adoption in lifestyle segments.  
- Weekend transactions generate higher average basket size, while weekdays drive higher transaction volume.  

---

## 🎯 Business Recommendations  

- Strengthen membership differentiation to increase customer lifetime value.  
- Implement category-specific payment promotions.  
- Leverage weekend high-basket behavior through bundling strategies.  
- Monitor branch-level performance for localized growth opportunities.
